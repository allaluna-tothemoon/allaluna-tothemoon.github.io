Privacy Policy for BabyTracker

Last updated: May 15, 2025

BabyTracker respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use the BabyTracker mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. All information entered into the app — such as baby feeding records, diaper changes, or profile details — remains on your device.

2. No Internet-Based Services

The app does not connect to external servers or cloud services. It operates entirely offline, and no data is transmitted over the internet.

3. No Analytics or Advertising Tracking

BabyTracker does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies of any kind.

4. Your Data Stays Private

All baby care information is stored locally on your device. We do not access or share this data with any third parties.
